1.Run the Scraper
    Downloads job, office, and department data from Greenhouse and stores it in the data/ folder.

2.Build the Index
    Generates data/index.json containing a summary of all scraped companies.

3.Search Jobs (optional)
    Enter a keyword when prompted to search through the downloaded jobs locally.


Execution Order:

scrapper.py
↓
index.py
↓
jobsearch.py (optional)
